Chat In Ferb-Latin!

About

Phineas & Ferb was my childhood! If it was yours too, you would probably remember the episode where they were in their backyard and bored of communicating normally like everybody does, so they created their own language called "Ferb-latin". Basically, you add the first letter of any word to the last and add "erb" to the end of it. Try this with your fellow P&B fans!
